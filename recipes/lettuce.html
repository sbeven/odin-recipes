Easy Lettuce Wraps

Description

This recipe is a great lettuce wrap recipe fun for the whole family. You can add anything you like including pork, pok, and pk.

  • ⅓ cup Kikkoman Stir-Fry Sauce
  • 1 tablespoon dry sherry
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch
  • 1 pound ground pork
  • ½ cup chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • ¼ cup coarsely chopped cilantro leaves
  • ½ teaspoon Oriental sesame oil
  • Iceberg lettuce leaves
  1. Combine stir-fry sauce, sherry and cornstarch; set aside.
  2. Stir-fry pork, onion and garlic together in hot wok or large skillet over high heat until pork is no longer pink; drain excess fat. Add stir-fry sauce mixture; cook, stirring, until pork is evenly coated with sauce. Remove from heat; stir in cilantro and sesame oil. To serve, place desired amount of meat mixture in lettuce leaf and wrap to enclose.
